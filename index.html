By accessing and using the PHARAONIX platform, you accept and agree to be bound by the terms and provision of this agreement. The platform provides job listing services connecting employers with job seekers in Egypt.

2. CV Review Fee

A non-refundable CV review fee is charged for each application. This fee covers the processing and review of your application by the employer. Fee amounts are displayed clearly on each job listing.

3. User Obligations

Users must provide accurate and truthful information in their applications. Any falsification of data may result in the rejection of applications and potential banning from the platform.

4. Payment Terms

All payments are processed securely through our supported payment methods. Once a payment is confirmed, it cannot be reversed. Users will receive a unique tracking code for their application.

5. Limitation of Liability

PHARAONIX acts as an intermediary platform and does not guarantee employment. We are not responsible for employer decisions, interview outcomes, or any disputes between employers and applicants.

Data Collection

We collect personal information you provide when applying for jobs: name, email, phone number, CV, and cover letter. Payment information is processed securely through third-party providers.

Data Usage

Your data is used exclusively for job application processing and communication regarding your applications. We do not sell or share your personal data with third parties for marketing purposes.

Data Storage

All personal data is stored securely with encryption. CV files are stored in protected directories accessible only to authorized personnel.

Data Retention

Application data is retained for 12 months after submission. You may request deletion of your data by contacting our support team.

Cookies

We use essential cookies for language preferences and session management. No tracking cookies are used without your explicit consent.
